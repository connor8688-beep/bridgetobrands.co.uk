Types of partnership

What brand
activations look like.

From a single product sampling afternoon to a full branded event series — we match the right activation type to your venue and your brand partner.

Product sampling

Brands distribute their products directly to your customers in your venue. Low effort for you, high value for the brand.

Branded pop-ups

A brand takes over a section of your space for a defined period — typically a weekend or a key date in their calendar.

Sponsored events

Brands sponsor your existing events — quiz nights, DJ sets, launch parties — in exchange for exposure to your audience.

Menu placements

A brand's product is featured on your menu — cocktails, food pairings, specials — with agreed promotion and signage.

Social content days

Brands use your venue as a backdrop for photo and video content — a paid shoot that earns you income with minimal disruption.

Venue takeovers

Full branded experience — décor, staff briefing, product exclusive for a night or a series. Our highest-value deal type.
